# Drop Out

## Prerequisites
- Currently enrolled in high school, college, or university
- No completion of higher education

## Stat and Economic Impacts
- Stops smarts gains from schooling
- Reduces access to higher-paying jobs
- Existing student loans continue accruing interest

## Advancement Paths
- Re-enroll in high school or higher education later
- Take GED exam to qualify for college
- Enter workforce with limited career growth

## Interactions
- **Education Level:** Sets status to dropped out, blocking advanced schooling until addressed
- **Loans:** Any outstanding loans remain and interest continues
- **Achievements:** Unlocks dropout-related achievement
